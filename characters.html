Sohpie Hatter

Sophie Hatter is the main character in the first book of the Castle Series, "Howl's Moving Castle," and its movie adaptation. Living in the small town of Market Chipping in the kingdom of Ingary, she's the oldest of three sisters. In Ingary, that usually means a dull life without much chance of finding fortune. While her sisters pursue different careers, Sophie works at her late father's hat shop, learning from her stepmother, Fanny Hatter.</p

Howl Pendragon

Howl Jenkins Pendragon is a formidable wizard residing in the enchanting realm of Ingary. Formerly Howell Jenkins of Wales, he belonged to a group of wizards on Earth. Venturing through a mystical portal to Ingary, Howl earned renown as one of the land's most esteemed sorcerers. He possesses a mobile castle and deliberately fosters rumors to safeguard his privacy, preferring solitude and evading any entanglements with conflict.

  

Calcifer

Calcifer is a Fire Demon bound by a magical pact with Wizard Howl. Once a falling star, Howl intercepted him before he plummeted to earth and vanished. While possessing considerable magical prowess, Calcifer is restricted to the hearth where Howl confines him, unable to move without the wizard's aid.

  

Markl

In the movie, Markl serves as Howl's apprentice. While his background remains largely mysterious, it's clear he's training under Howl to become a wizard. In Howl's absence, Markl handles his master's affairs, attending to customers in various towns connected by portals.

His magical abilities are limited, with his most notable feat being the transformation into a short, elderly wizard when he dons a cloak. In this guise, his voice adopts a huskier, deeper tone.

  

Wicked Witch of the Waste

The Waste Witch emerges as the primary antagonist in the book "Howl's Moving Castle" and as a secondary antagonist in its film adaptation. A skilled witch renowned for her beauty, she once captivated Howl's attention. However, upon discovering her use of magic to maintain her youth and allure, Howl severed ties with her. Despite his rejection, she remains enamored with him and employs her magical prowess and minions in an attempt to capture him after spotting him with Sophie.

  

Turnip Head

Turnip Head, credited as Turnip in the film and known as Prince Justin in the original novels, is a character in "Howl's Moving Castle." After Sophie saves him during her journey to the wasteland, he becomes her steadfast companion. Under a curse, Turnip Head takes the form of a scarecrow dressed in a black suit, sporting a top hat with a turnip for a head, and moves by hopping like a sprung coil. When restored to his princely form, he appears as a tall, blond man dressed in a yellow suit, complete with a top hat and a pink bowtie.
